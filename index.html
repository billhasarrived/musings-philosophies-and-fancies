About Bill
I'm a 21 y/o male with no life to speak of as of current, I have a background in tech, and an uncanny ability to learn technical things quickly.
I have loved Star Wars for my entire life, but at the moment it's a simmering love/hate relationship. I just finished The Wheel of Time book series, and it was probably the most gloriously human thing I've ever read, and I don't think I'll be able to look at anything the same way now. I have a vested interest in NCIS, I'm currently on season 9 on a backlog watch, and I've seen the three most recent seasons as they have come out, along with NCIS Origins as it has released.
I yearn for old school skeuomorphic or Y2K design, and despise flat design with a passion. I am probably THE definition of the term 'power user', and it annoys me to no end when something has the ability to do something but doesn't expose it.
I used to be a avid gamer, but the times have forced me to accept that it's not as viable as it once was, and as it stands now, I play Minecraft and Lego Games mostly.

Fun Fact
